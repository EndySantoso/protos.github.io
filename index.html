About Company

PT. Menara Medika Pratama, which is more common called MMP by our customers, is a glove distribution company, named Protos as their brand name.

PT. Menara Medika Pratama, was established under Notarial Deed of H.Feby Rubein Hidayat, SH No.82 on 8 th October 2009, and was legalized by the Minister of Law and Human Right through a letter Surat Keputusan No. AHU-50949.AH.01.01 on the 21 th of October 2009. Our company believes that we are able to market our business locally and internationally. To complete the trust of our product, PROTOS has been registered in the Direktorat Jenderal Pelayanan Kefarmasian dan Alat Kesehatan, and legalized on the 20 th of September 2005.
